Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about transfers, wallet funding, withdrawals, fees, and account security.

Support: support@KoinSave.com

How fast are transactions?

Transfers between KoinSave users are instant. Bank withdrawals typically take 1–10 minutes depending on your bank network.

Funding Your Wallet

You can fund your KoinSave wallet using debit card, bank transfer, or via a verified merchant. All deposits reflect immediately.

Is My Money Safe?

Yes. We use bank-grade encryption, two-factor authentication, and device monitoring to protect every transaction on your account.

Transaction Fees

Sending money to another KoinSave user is free. Bank withdrawals and merchant payments include small processing fees which are clearly shown before you confirm any transaction.

Reversals & Disputes

If you made a mistake or sent money to the wrong user, you may request a reversal within 5 minutes of the transaction. Our support team will review and update you promptly.

Privacy Policy

Your data is encrypted and never shared with third parties. Only essential information needed to process your transactions is stored securely.
